About

Hi there! I’m Yi-Ming Ding (丁一茗), a PhD student currently at Fudan University and Westlake University.

My research sits at the theoretical and numerical studies of Quantum Information and Quantum Many-Body Physics.

I’m particularly obsessed with how information-theoretical proxies such as entanglement and nonstabilizerness (magic) characterize different many-body systems and fruitful collective behaviors.

Besides, I enjoy coding and am also a passionate Rustacean.
